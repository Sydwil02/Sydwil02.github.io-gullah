How to Get Here

From I-95: Take exit 8 to merge onto US 278E/Independence Blvd. toward Beaufort – 2.1 mi Continue onto William Hilton Parkway – 0.5 mi Turn right onto Gumtree Road – 0.1 mi Turn right onto Honey Horn Drive – 0.2 mi Arrive at Gullah Heritage Trail Tours 70 Honey Horn Plantation Rd, Hilton Head Island, SC, 29926 Gullah Heritage Trail Tours picks up and drops off at this Coastal Discovery Museum location.
