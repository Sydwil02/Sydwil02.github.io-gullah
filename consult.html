Consulting & Educational Outreach

Gullah Heritage Consulting Services is one of the leading providers of professional services, products, programs, and consulting regarding the history, culture, and preservation of Gullah Heritage. Dr. Emory Shaw Campbell is the main consultant of this five member firm devoted to helping individuals, organizations, groups, journalists and scholars learn more about the Gullah Cultural Heritage. While the Tour is our primary venue for edutainment, we aim to offer other opportunities for a wider audience to learn about GullahGeechee cultural heritage. Our senior Guide is considered a foremost expert on GullahGeechee culture. Under the leadership of this expert, our Tour staff is uniquely proficient in presenting the broad and specific facts on the culture to a diverse audience. Furthermore, we stand ready to dispatch expert presentations and/or advice to writers, academics, family reunions or any of various interested persons or groups.
